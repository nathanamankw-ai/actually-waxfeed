# TasteID Archetype Classification System
## Comprehensive Music Listener Typology

---

## Overview

The TasteID Archetype System classifies listeners into distinct taste profiles based on two orthogonal classification methods:
1. **Genre-Based Archetypes**: What you listen to
2. **Behavioral Archetypes**: How you listen

Each user receives a Primary and Secondary archetype, creating 400+ unique combinations.

---

## Scientific Basis

### Music Preference Research

**Reference:** Sch√§fer, T., & Sedlmeier, P. (2009). From the functions of music to music preference. *Psychology of Music*, 37(3), 279-300.

Three fundamental functions of music listening:
1. **Self-awareness** (identity, emotional regulation)
2. **Social relatedness** (belonging, cultural identity)
3. **Arousal/Mood regulation** (stimulation, relaxation)

### Listener Type Research

**Reference:** Greasley, A. E., & Lamont, A. (2011). Exploring engagement with music in everyday life using experience sampling methodology. *Musicae Scientiae*, 15(1), 45-71.

Identified listener engagement styles:
- **Casual listeners**: Background music, genre-agnostic
- **Engaged listeners**: Active attention, selective
- **Enthusiasts**: Deep engagement, collectors
- **Musicians**: Technical appreciation, theory-aware

## Part 3: POLARITY √ó Archetype Matrix

### Primary Detection Algorithm (v3.0 - Adaptive Confidence)

```
FOR each user:
  1. Cluster genres into FAMILIES (based on musicological taxonomy)
  2. Compute family distribution: dominant_share, top2_share, distinct_count
  3. Compute max_confidence from review_count (psychometric scaling)
  4. Score all genre-based archetypes (fuzzy matching)
  5. Evaluate behavioral archetype criteria
  
  CLASSIFICATION DECISION TREE:
  
  IF dominant_family_share > 0.30:
    ‚Üí STRONG GENRE signal
    ‚Üí primary = best genre archetype
    ‚Üí confidence = 0.70 + (dominance - 0.30) * 0.5
    
  ELIF dominant_family_share > 0.20:
    ‚Üí MODERATE GENRE signal
    ‚Üí primary = best genre archetype
    ‚Üí confidence = 0.60 + (dominance - 0.20) * 0.5
    
  ELIF meets_genre_fluid_criteria():
    ‚Üí TRUE GENRE FLUID
    ‚Üí primary = 'genre-fluid'
    ‚Üí confidence = fluid_score
    
  ELSE:
    ‚Üí DEFAULT to best genre match
    
  FINALLY:
    ‚Üí Cap confidence at max_confidence (data sufficiency)
  
  Genre Fluid STRICT Criteria:
    - adventureness > 0.50 (after dominance penalties)
    - 5+ distinct families
    - no single family > 30%
    - top 2 families combined < 50%
```

Based on psychometric reliability research (Nunnally & Bernstein, 1994):
